Description

Early Life & Education

Vernon was born on February 18,1998 in Manhattan,New York.He was born to Korean father and French-American.He has a younger sister Sofia who born on 2004.He grew up in New York until his family moved to South Korea when he was 5 years old.After moving to South Korea,he lived in Seoul and attended regular schools.Vernon graduated from Daebu Elementary School,a prestigious private Elementary school.he was raised in the Hongdae neighborhood.So, he was growing up surrounded by the Korean culture since he was a child.He said that when he was young,he had a hard time because people would stare at him because of his exoitc mixed-race appearances.Due to his busy training schedule,he dropped-off middle school with his parent's consent and was home-schooled since then.His childhood dream was to be a movie actor or movie production team.

Career

Since he was child, he was casted in many children's TV broadcasts and also appeared in the variety show 'Song Euni's Eye to Eye' as a love master elementary school student.Vernon's parents,who saw their son enjoying his appearance in TV broadcasts, decided to fully support Vernon's wish to work in the entertainment field.He was street-casted by Pledis's staff in a bus stop on his way back to home from school.He got cast by Pledis Entertaiment at a train station in front of his middle school.He trained for three years and three months before making his debut with Seventeen.Before his debut, he appeared in Man of K's "Falling" music video,Hello Venus' "Vernus" music video,and Orange Caramrl's "My Copycat" music video.In the early debut , he used the catchphrase '4D Rapper' when introducing himself.He has the highest tone among the other hip-hop team members.Vernon is actively participating in writing and composing SEVENTEEN songs.As of FErbruary 2021, he got 68 songs registered under his name.The second most credited member in SEVENTEEN.He officially released two solo songs 'Lotto' in 2015 and 'Disease' in 2016.Vernon joined Mnet's survival program Show Me The Money 4, where he got a lot of criticism from the judges and fellow contestants of the show.
